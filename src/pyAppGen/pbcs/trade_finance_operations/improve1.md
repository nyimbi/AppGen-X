# Trade Finance Operations PBC Improvement Backlog

## Purpose

This backlog identifies 50 high-impact, high-value improvements for `trade_finance_operations`. Each item is specific to letters of credit, guarantees, documentary collections, sanctions checks, shipment documents, and trade settlement and is intended to move the package toward complete domain coverage.
