Nyimbi Odero is a renowned technology leader on the African continent, with extensive experience in designing, developing, deploying, and operating multifarious national-scale technical projects. He has had notable successes in the areas of cloud computing, artificial intelligence, machine learning, IoT, blockchain, and systems engineering. Nyimbi is widely recognized as a leader in Cloud FinOps, and IT Governance.  Nyimbi, a widely published practitioner, is generally considered a global expert on electoral systems, cyber security, large scale project and intervention design and management, and data analytics. His current interests are in cryptocurrency, natural language processing, countering violent extremism, governance innovation and automated information assurance.

Nyimbi's experience spans both the public and private sectors, where he has served in various leadership roles. He has held senior positions such as the Head of West Africa at Google, CEO of Seme Research, and SVP of Technical Product Management at Crossover. In the public sector, Nyimbi has served as Advisor for Innovation and Digitization to the Government of Kenya, CTO at the Independent National Electoral Commission in Nigeria, Advisor to the Chief Justice of Kenya, and Advisor to the Ghana Judiciary and Police Service. Nyimbi is a successful publisher and holds a B.Sc. in Theoretical Physics from the University of Nairobi,  and an ACE in Management and Leadership from MIT. Nyimbi Odero is based in Kenya.

Overall, Nyimbi Odero's extensive experience in both the public and private sectors, along with his many successes in designing and implementing deeply technical projects, positions him as a leading technology expert on the African continent.
